## Market Types

- **Lending** - Deposit and borrow assets with real-time APY rates
- **Yield Aggregator** (soon) - Automated yield optimization strategies
- **Staking** (soon) - SOL and asset staking with reward tracking
- **Loop** - Leveraged position strategies (also called looping)
- **Perps** - Perpetual futures and leverage products
- **Prediction** - Bet on outcomes of real-world events
- **Market Making** (soon) - AMM, CLMM, and DLMM liquidity provision
- **Perps Liquidity Providing** (soon) - Perpetual futures liquidity strategies

To understand the different market types, as well as the supported protocols, and attributes, please refer to the [Market Types Section](/markets/market-types) page.
