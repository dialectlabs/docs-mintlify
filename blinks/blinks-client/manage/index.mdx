# Manage

Manage your Blinks lifecycle, including deployment, updates, versioning, and maintenance operations.

## Overview

Effective Blinks management ensures your blockchain actions remain functional, secure, and optimized. This includes handling updates, monitoring health, and managing user access.

## Management Features

- **Deployment Management**: Deploy and update your Blinks across different environments
- **Version Control**: Manage different versions of your Blinks and rollback capabilities
- **Health Monitoring**: Track the operational status of your Blinks
- **Access Control**: Manage who can interact with your Blinks
- **Configuration Updates**: Update Blinks settings without redeployment

## Lifecycle Management

### Development
- Local testing and validation
- Integration with development tools
- Debugging and troubleshooting

### Staging
- Pre-production testing
- User acceptance testing
- Performance validation

### Production
- Live deployment
- Monitoring and alerts
- Maintenance and updates
